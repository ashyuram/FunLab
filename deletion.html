Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

In compliance with Facebook Platform Policies, we provide instructions on how users can request the deletion of their personal information and game activity data associated with Stack Color Rush.

Method 1. Automated Deletion via Facebook Settings (Recommended)

You can remove the Game's permission to access your data and wipe the active server connection at any time by following these steps:

1 Go to your Facebook profile and click Settings & Privacy.

2 Navigate to Apps and Websites in the left sidebar menu.

3 Locate Stack Color Rush in the list of active integrations.

4 Click the Remove button next to the game name.

5 Confirm by checking the option to delete game history and click Remove again.

Method 2. Manual Data Deletion Request via Email

If you prefer to have all your database records (including high scores, leaderboard stats, and profile linking records) permanently and manually expunged, please send us a request via email:

📧 Support Email: ashyuram@gmail.com

💡 In your email, please provide your **Facebook Display Name** or **Facebook User ID**. Upon verification, we will permanently purge all database records associated with your account within 24-48 hours and send you a confirmation reply.
