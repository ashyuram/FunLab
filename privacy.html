Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

We develop the HTML5 game Stack Color Rush (the "Game") as an Instant Game. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle the limited user information accessed via the Facebook Instant Games platform.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide you with a functional game experience and competitive leaderboards, the Game accesses the following information via the Facebook Instant Games SDK:

  • Facebook User ID: A platform-specific unique identifier used to load and save your game progress and high scores.
  • Display Name & Profile Picture: Used solely to display your identity in the active game UI and competitive friend leaderboards.

Note: We do not collect or request sensitive personal information such as email addresses, phone numbers, or friend lists without your explicit additional consent.
